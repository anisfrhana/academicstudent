Overview of Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is a prominent public university in Malaysia, committed to providing high-quality education and transforming students into leaders in various fields. Established in 1956, UiTM offers comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines including technology, business, engineering, law, and social sciences.

With over 30 campuses spread throughout Malaysia, UiTM has become one of the largest higher education institutions in the country. It is well-known for its emphasis on practical education, skill development, research-driven teaching, and commitment to promoting access to education for all Malaysians.

As a world-class institution, UiTM continues to nurture talent, foster innovation, and contribute significantly to national development by producing graduates who are prepared for the workforce, entrepreneurship, and advanced research endeavors.

Key Features of UiTM

  • Founded: 1956
  • Campuses: 30+ campuses nationwide
  • Programs Offered: A wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs
  • Accreditation: Internationally recognized for its high academic standards
  • Research Excellence: Strong focus on research and development in various fields

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a premier institution that provides innovative and globally recognized education and research.

Mission: To develop well-rounded individuals with high ethical standards, capable of leading and excelling in various fields, and contributing positively to society.
