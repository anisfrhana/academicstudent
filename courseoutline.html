Course Outline

University Teknologi MARA (UiTM) - Course Information

Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons) Computer Networks

Course Overview:

This program focuses on the design, development, and management of computer networks and related technologies.

Course Content:
  • Computer Network Fundamentals
  • Network Design and Architecture
  • Wireless Networks
  • Network Security
  • Cloud Computing and Virtualization
Learning Outcomes:
  • Understand the principles of computer networks
  • Design secure networks
  • Implement and troubleshoot network systems

Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) Information Systems Management

Course Overview:

This program focuses on the management of information systems within organizations to ensure their strategic use and improvement.

Course Content:
  • Systems Development Life Cycle
  • Project Management in IT
  • Information Systems Analysis and Design
  • Enterprise Resource Planning
  • IT Governance and Ethics
Learning Outcomes:
  • Develop and manage information systems
  • Apply project management skills in IT projects
  • Ensure effective governance of IT resources

Bachelor of Information Systems (Hons) Business Computing

Course Overview:

This course prepares students to apply computing solutions in business environments, helping organizations optimize their processes.

Course Content:
  • Business Process Modelling
  • Data Analytics for Business
  • Enterprise Systems Integration
  • Database Management Systems
  • Business Intelligence and Decision Support Systems
Learning Outcomes:
  • Implement IT solutions to solve business problems
  • Analyze and interpret business data
  • Manage business-related IT projects

Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) Information Content Management

Course Overview:

This program is designed to focus on managing, organizing, and distributing information and digital content.

Course Content:
  • Digital Content Management
  • Web Development and Design
  • Content Management Systems
  • Information Retrieval and Search Engines
  • Knowledge Management
Learning Outcomes:
  • Manage and optimize content for the web
  • Design and implement content management systems
  • Understand the role of digital content in knowledge management
